Welcome to the Melody Machine: for basic melodic dictation!

♪ Step 1: Select your levels for pitch and rhythm and press "Generate!" Your exercise will appear below.
♪ Step 2: Click the "Listen" button. You will hear a one measure melody in 4/4 time.
♪ Step 3: Click the box which contains the correct answer. It will be highlighted for you.
♪ Step 4: Press the submit button to check your answer, and keep practicing! You can choose to continue with the same level, or generate from a new one.
