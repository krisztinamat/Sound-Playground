Harmony Machine: Two-Part Pitch Dictation Generator


♪ Step 1: Select your levels for melody and harmony, then press "Generate!" Your exercise will appear below.
♪ Step 2: Click the "Listen" button. You will hear two voices at the SAME time, each sounding 4 notes.
♪ Step 3: Click the box which contains the correct answer. It will be highlighted for you.
♪ Step 4: Press the submit button to check your answer, and keep practicing! You can choose to continue with the same level, or generate from a new one.

Note: these exercises do not follow rules of good voice leading, rather they are for students to practice listening to independent parts.
