Table of Rhythms: Review Guide

When the quarter note is worth one beat, these rhythms (and their corresponding rests) have the following values:

Rhythm Value
୦ (whole note) 4 beats
ᑻ (dotted half note) 3 beats
ᑯ (half note) 2 beats
♩. (dotted quarter note) 1 and 1/2 beats
♩ (quarter note) 1 beat
♪. (dotted eighth note) 3/4 of a beat
♪ (eighth note) 1/2 of a beat
ᚫ (sixteenth note) 1/4 of a beat
